4+ Years Of Experience

'The Compass Interior' stans for all Direction innovation and professionalism.

Their team consists designers who consistently work under the vision of Ashish Prajapati.

Ashish Prajapati is the founder and chief interior designer of ‘The Compass Interior’, a leading Design firm based in Jamnagar, Gujarat.They aslo deal in residential, commercial and hospitality interior design solutions in various parts of Gujarat in they are working on projects even at Jamnagar, Rajkot and Ahemdabad.

At ‘The Compass Interior’, their Motto is ‘We Listen, Create and You Enjoy’.

They aim towards creating soul soothing interior spaces that reflect the personality and identity of their clients with their creativity.

At 'The Compass Interior' believe that Interior Design is no more mere a luxury. It is the need of the time. The space that a person resides in or works in strongly determines the moods, productivity and feelings of the person.

We work with a vision of the space and further design it with all aesthetic and functional aspects needed.

Mission Is Design & Develop The Best Interior

Our mission is to ENHANCE YOUR QUALITY OF LIFE THROUGH INTERIOR DESIGN. We understand that the look and feel of your home and workplace can affect your behavior, attitude and sense of well-being.

Our mission is to positively influence the lives of all customers with whom we have the pleasure of working, and leave a lasting impression on their minds and imagination.
